## English

zipax is a small open source desktop app for compressing images and PDFs. If it saves you time, reduces a repetitive workflow, or helps you ship smaller files, you can support ongoing maintenance with a small donation.

Your support helps with:

- Polishing the desktop UI and tray experience.
- Improving compression quality and format coverage.
- Maintaining macOS, Windows, and Linux builds.
- Keeping the project documented and approachable for contributors.

## 中文

zipax 是一个开源的桌面端图片和 PDF 压缩工具。如果它帮你节省了一点时间、减少了一些重复操作，或者让文件变得更容易分享，欢迎通过小额赞助支持后续维护。

你的支持会用于：

- 持续打磨桌面 UI 和菜单栏体验。
- 改进压缩质量和格式支持。
- 维护 macOS、Windows、Linux 构建。
- 完善文档，让项目更容易被使用和贡献。
